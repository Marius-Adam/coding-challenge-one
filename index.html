Casement windows

Our A rated windows come with soft coat low E 24mm glazed units
providing excellent thermal efficiency with a u value of 1.4 W/m2K. The
hardwearing 70mm uPVC frame is virtually maintenance free and won't
rot, flake, peel or rust. They're also internally beaded for added security
and come with a secure locking system. Each window complies with
Building Regulations Part L and Part F and Scottish Section 6.

Key features

With our uPVC casement windows, you don’t have to compromise on
style or function. Our windows offer a beautiful, adaptable appearance,
alongside modern levels of security, energy efficiency and
weatherproofing.

  • Drainage channels in frame to carry rain away
  • Secured by Design profile with secure, multipoint locking
  • Low maintenance materials that don't require painting
  • Available in a wide range of colours and finishes
  • Chambered frame and sash to trap warm air
